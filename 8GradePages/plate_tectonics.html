Portfolio | Plate Tectonics Project

What was the Plate Tectonics Project?

The plate tectonics project was a project that required us to create a model of the tectonic movement at a certain location on earth.


How did I grow in my craftsmanship during this project?

One of the requirements of the project was to have smooth placement of duct tape on our model. I was not very good at placing duct tape smoothly before the project, but I definently became much better at doing this at the end of the project. I also became much better at using tools like Dremels and Hot Wire cutters.
