Experiences

The main extra-curricular activity I do is Robotics. I am part of the DRSS Varsity Robotics Team. The Robotics Competition we participate in is FIRST Tech Challenge. FIRST is also a PBL Based organization that encourages innovative design. I have been doing Robotics for 3 years, and I currently do Engineering, Programming, and Computer Aided Design. Here are some pictures of my robot from last year. The second image is a CAD model which I helped make. The main portion of the robot I worked on was the white shielding you see attached to the robot in both images. This required the use of our laser cutter. Being on the team, I am experienced in using high tech manufacturing such as 3D-Printing, and laser cutting.
